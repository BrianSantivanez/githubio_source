IBM Bluemix for AWS professionals

This article will hopefully help anyone coming from the AWS ecosystem translate their workloads into IBM Bluemix.

Basic Concepts

Bluemix has three main silos of services. Infrastructure , Applications , and Services.

Infrastructure is built on SoftLayer and is available in over 30 SoftLayer Datacenters across the world. All of which are interconnect with our unmetered private network.

Applications can be either Cloud Foundry, Docker, or Open Whisk

Services contain a huge catalog of pay as you go addons to your application.

What follows is a rough comaprison between Bluemix, AWS and Azure. Not all of these services will line up 1:1, but most of them are fairly close. Of course, if you have questions about anything in here, please Contact Us
